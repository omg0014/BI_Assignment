Bug #2 · High
Paise / INR Conversion Missing or Wrong
Wage rates are stored in INR (e.g. ₹300/hr) but bank transfers are in paise (30000 paise = ₹300). The reconciliation multiplies by 100 only if max rate > 1000 — a heuristic that may fail on new rate tables. One worker has a Feb delta of ₹2.1L which is almost certainly a conversion artifact, not a real underpayment.
